Burger

Description

Need a fast and easy burger recipe? This recipe takes 10 ingredients and 20 minutes and serves 4. Learn how to make your own burgers quickly!

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 2 tomatos
  • 1 onion
  • ketchup
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grill.
  2. Whisk egg, salt and black pepper together in a bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs to the mixture and mix together with your hands until well blended.
  4. Form mixture into four 3/4 inch patties.
  5. Cook on grill for 6 - 8 minutes a side or until preferred doneness. Internal temperature should be 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Slice toppings and assemble, enjoy!
