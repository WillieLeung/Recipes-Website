Chicken and Waffle Bites

Description

Need a delicious breakfast recipe? This chicken and waffle bites recipe takes 3 ingredients and 30 minutes and serves 6. Lightly breaded chicken tenders are dipped in pancake batter, and popped into a waffle iron until they are crisp and golden.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.6 ounces) jug shake and pour pancake mix
  • 1 1/2 pounds frozen fully cooked lightly breaded chicken tenders
  • cooking spray
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup, or as needed for dipping

Steps

  1. Prepare chicken tenders using package instructions.
  2. Prepare pancake mix using package instructions.
  3. Heat up a waffle iron and spray with cooking spray. Take 1 - 4 chicken tenders depending on size of tenders/iron, dip into pancake batter then cook on waffle iron until golden brown.
  4. Repeat for remaining chicken tenders.
  5. Serve hot with maple syrup as the dipping sauce, enjoy!
